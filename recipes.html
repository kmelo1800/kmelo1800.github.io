Chocolate chip and walnut brownies

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1/3 cup olive oil
  • 1 cup Nessie chocolate chip
  • 1 cup sweetened cocoa powder
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup chopped walnuts

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Pam butter a 9"x9" square baking pan
  3. Melt butter and chocolate chips together
  4. With a mixing bowl, combine the dry ingredients
  5. When the chocolate chip mix has cooled whisk it up with the eggs
  6. Add the dry ingredients with the chocolate chip/egg mix and whisk!
  7. Slowly add in the chopped walnuts while mixing
  8. Pour the batter into pre buttered baking pan
  9. Bake for about 30 minutes
  10. ENJOY!
